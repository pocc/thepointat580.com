 The Point at 580 is located at the highway on-ramp of Interstate 580, just east of the Richmond-San Rafael Bridge, south of the Richmond Parkway, and two blocks from the center of historic Point Richmond.   The property features storefront shops along Tewksbury Avenue, professional offices, warehouse/office spaces and single family houses.  ​Olivia and James Jacobs run an environmental and engineering consulting company and have owned and managed the property since 1996.  The owners welcome responsible tenants with pets.  We even offer virtual offices for​small businesses not wanting to commit to a lease but needing services.
